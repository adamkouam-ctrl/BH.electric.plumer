# Vanguard Flow & Spark — Elettricista & Idraulico Nord Italia

> Vanguard Flow & Spark is a certified electrical and plumbing service company operating across northern Italy. Available 7 days a week, 07:00–21:00, with same-day emergency response, transparent pricing, and a single point of contact.

## Services

- **Impianti Elettrici** — Installation, maintenance and repair of residential and commercial electrical systems; electrical panels, wiring, earthing, sockets, switches, certification.
- **Impianti Idraulici** — Water leak detection and repair, tap and mixer replacement, installation of toilets/showers/basins, drainage and waste pipe management.
- **Interventi Urgenti** — Emergency response for power outages, sudden leaks, blocked drains; urgent weekend availability.
- **Ristrutturazioni** — Full electrical and plumbing fit-out for renovations; turnkey bathrooms and kitchens; DICO certification at completion.
